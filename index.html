Rishi Upadhyay

I am a 1st year PhD student at UCLA where I am advised by Prof. Achuta Kadambi. My research interests include computer vision and computer graphics, particularly diffusion models and causal learning.

I attended UC Berkeley for my undergrad where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Ren Ng and Prof. Avideh Zakhor.
